My AI policy in this website

I don't really have a defined AI policy for my website. I say "manually updated with love" on the bottom of my site because i edit the files on html myself on replit, instead of using some WYSIWYG editor, but despite that claim, I've used AI on some of my pages:

  • My list of unfinished business was made using ChatGPT (GPT-4) from some bulletpoints I gave . I don't say "manually updated with love" in that one tho.
  • My life story was made using AI to transcribe the text (Deepgram). It's a video transcript that hasn't been manually revised (I think it's obvious). I think that one does say "manually updated with love", even tho it was mostly copying and pasting, because there's something I've written at the beginning IIRC. Plus I spoke those words, I guess.

My AI policy in general

I don't like AI generated text often, the list of unfinished business was made with AI and I can easily tell that it doesn't sound like me. But it is a list of unfinished business, so it's unpolished on purpose.

But I use AI often to talk to it and ask it questions, I've talked to ChatGPT (GPT4 and 4o), Claude (3.5) and lately Perplexity Pro to ask both "general knowledge"/curiosity related questions, but also to "talk stuff out" with personal issues. I also think it's useful for languages, translating stuff like song lyrics from Japanese, and I plan to use it for writing emails in German to get jobs...
